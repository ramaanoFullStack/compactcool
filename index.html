Compact Cool Cooling Unit Manufacturers (Pty) Ltd was established in South Africa in 1976. It operates from its own factory in the south of Johannesburg in a 4000m2 premises. The company manufactures and supplies industrial cooling equipment for air and water cooling applications. Currently over 37 personnel are employed with most having been with the company for over 20 years.
